Privacy Policy

This privacy policy governs the use of the HRMS (Human Resource Management System) Application.

Permissions

The HRMS Application requires the following permissions:

Camera

The HRMS Application may require access to your device's camera in order to capture images for various features. This permission is necessary to enable functionalities such as capturing photos scanning QR codes. We assure you that we respect your privacy and will only access the camera when explicitly required by the application. We do not store or transmit any media captured by the camera without your explicit consent.

Location

The HRMS Application may request access to your device's location information to provide location-based features. This permission is necessary to enable functionalities such as recording employee attendance or suggesting nearby office locations. We assure you that we respect your privacy and will only access your location data when explicitly required by the application. We do not share your location information with any third parties without your explicit consent.

Application features

The HRMS Application provides the following features:

Leave Attendance

The application allows employees to mark their attendance for leaves, providing an easy and convenient way to manage leave records.

Leave Request

Employees can submit leave requests through the application, which enables efficient leave management and approval processes.

Overtime

The application allows employees to record and track their overtime hours, facilitating accurate overtime calculations and compensation.

Sale Activity

The application provides functionality to record and monitor sales activities, assisting in sales tracking and performance analysis.
